  1. N は place です
    Using this sentence pattern, you can state where a place, thing or person is.
    • The toilet is over there.
      トイレはあそこです。
    • Mr.Yamada is in his office.
      山田さんは事務所です。
  2. N1 の N2
    When N1 is the name of a country and N2 is the name of a product, it means that N2 is made in that country.
    When N1 is the name of a company and N2 is the name of a product, it means that N2 is made by that company.
  3. V ます/V ません/V ました/V ませんでした
    V ます is used when a sentence express something habitual or a truth. It is also used when a sentence expresses behaviour or event that will occur in the future.The table below shows its negative and past-tense forms.
    Non-past(future/present)Past
    AffirmativeV ますV ました
    NegativeV ませんV ませんでした
  4. N(time) に V
    The particle に is appended to a noun indicating time to indicate the time of occurence of an action.
  5. N1 と N2
    The particle と connects two nouns in coordinate relation.
