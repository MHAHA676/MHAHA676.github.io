ひらがな/平仮名

Hiragana is used for various grammatical and function words, as well as furigana which serve as reading aid for kanji.

カタカナ/片仮名

Katakana are derived from components of kanji, used for foreign words transcription and some particular terms.

拗音

Yoon is a feature of the Japanese language in which a mora is formed with an added sound.
